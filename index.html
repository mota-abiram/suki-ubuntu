About the Project

Sukhii Ubuntu — Designed for Living, Made for Belonging

Spread across 5.25 lush acres in Puppalguda, Sukhii Ubuntu is a landmark residential community comprising 3 towers of 36 storeys each, home to 855 thoughtfully designed apartments. Every single unit is a corner flat, no shared walls, abundant natural light, and unobstructed views.

Rooted in the African philosophy of Ubuntu — "I am because we are" — this development is not just a residence. It is a neighbourhood designed to celebrate life together.

Why Ubuntu

What Sets Us Apart

Every detail at Sukhii Ubuntu is designed to elevate the way you live, breathe, and belong. Here's what makes it unlike anything else in Hyderabad.

100% Corner Flats

Not a single shared wall. Every apartment at Ubuntu is a corner flat, ensuring cross-ventilation, natural light from multiple directions, sprawling views, and complete privacy without isolation.

Connected Sky Terraces

All three towers are architecturally linked at the top, creating Hyderabad’s first sky deck, a community space in the sky fostering neighbourhood living at an elevated level.

IGBC Platinum Pre-Rated

India's highest green building certification. Superior energy efficiency, water conservation, and reduced carbon emissions, lowering your long-term utility costs.

Award-Winning Sustainability

Recognised as Best Eco-Friendly Sustainable Project — Times Business 2024. Natural ventilation design, eco-friendly roofing, and EV charging stations built in.

Prime Location

At Puppalguda — just 2 minutes from the Financial District, 15 minutes from HITEC City, and 0.5km from the Outer Ring Road. At the epicentre of West Hyderabad's growth corridor.

FAQs

Questions Answered

What is the RERA registration number?+
Sukhii Ubuntu is fully RERA-registered under ID: P02400003677 under the Telangana Real Estate Regulatory Authority, ensuring complete buyer protection and legal transparency.
What configurations are available?+
Ubuntu offers 2 BHK (1315 sq.ft), 3 BHK (1710 sq.ft), and 4 BHK (2230 sq.ft) apartments. All units are 100% corner flats with cross-ventilation and Vastu-compliant layouts.
What is the price range?+
Prices start from ₹1.5 Cr for a 2 BHK and go up to ₹2.5 Crore+ for a 4 BHK. Overall price range: ₹1.5 Cr – ₹2.5 Cr+ onwards.
When is possession expected?+
Possession is from 2026 onwards. Please contact our team for the latest tower-wise handover schedule.
What makes Ubuntu eco-friendly?+
Ubuntu is IGBC Platinum pre-rated — the highest green certification in India. It features solar water heaters, EV charging stations, a sewage treatment plant, rainwater harvesting, natural ventilation design, and eco-friendly roofing.
What is the Skydeck?+
At 400 feet above ground, the interconnected terraces of all three towers merge to form an entire world in the sky — with Community Gardens, a Party Lawn, Jogging Track, Mini Golf, Community Dining, Nectar & Flowering Gardens, a Yoga Court, and Reading Corners. It is Hyderabad's first skydeck, and the only one you can truly live in.
Who is the finance partner for this project?+
Sukhii Ubuntu is funded and mortgaged with Tata Capital — one of India's most trusted financial institutions. This is a strong marker of project credibility, financial discipline, and delivery commitment.
