When you visit our website, we may collect the following data:

How We Use Your Data

We collect your data for several reasons:

  • To provide our services to you

Safeguarding and Securing the Data

Sukhii Group is committed to securing your data and keeping it confidential. We have taken measures to prevent data theft, unauthorised access, and disclosure. We retain your information throughout our business relationship and afterward to maintain business records for audit purposes.

Your Right to Privacy

You have the right to:

We do not store data about children under the age of 13 as per our policy.
